Aeromodelling is the art of designing,building and flying miniaturized aircrafts (powered or non-powered).
One can build a model plane from any material like Paper, Balsa wood etc. It is both a hobby and sport;
the hobby aspect involves building and assembling model aircraft, and the sport part involves the flying.
To showcase their passion for flying the club members participate in various fares like techspardha,
held at Nit KKR and similar shows held at other institutes all over the country.
